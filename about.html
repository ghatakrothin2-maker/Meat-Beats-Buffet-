From the market stall to your kitchen

Taza Bazar started as a single butcher's counter in Gulshan and grew into a daily-price online market trusted across Dhaka.

Our Story

Honest prices, sourced fresh every morning

We built Taza Bazar because online grocery prices rarely reflected what was actually happening in the market that day. Our weekly price system mirrors the real bazar — when supply is good, prices drop; when it's tight, we say so upfront instead of hiding it in delivery fees.

Every item is sourced the same morning it's listed, from farms and fisheries we've worked with for years.
